Luca Hayes Content Platform

A creator productivity platform designed to help TikTok creators securely connect their accounts, manage content workflows, preview uploads, configure publishing settings and prepare content for publishing through TikTok integrations.

Platform Features

Secure TikTok Authentication

Creators connect their TikTok accounts through TikTok Login Kit using TikTok's official authorization flow.

Creator Information

The platform retrieves creator account information after authentication to identify the connected TikTok account.

Video Upload Workflow

Creators can upload video content, preview files and configure publishing settings before submission.

Publishing Preparation

Users can prepare content, review privacy settings and configure publishing options before initiating a publishing request.

About The Platform

Luca Hayes Content Platform is a web-based creator tool built to support content management and publishing workflows for TikTok creators. The platform integrates with TikTok Login Kit and TikTok APIs to provide secure account authentication and creator functionality.
